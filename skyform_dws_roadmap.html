How the Digital DWS System Works
A complete walkthrough of every step — from the engineer's morning scan to the verified record in the ops database.
Every green chip represents a passive data point captured automatically without the engineer needing to do anything extra.
1
Start of Day — Engineer Arrival
Morning
Engineer
🔑
QR Card Scan — Pier or Site Arrival
Engineer scans their personal laminated QR card using their phone camera. Takes 3 seconds. The check-in page opens automatically.
GPS captured Device timestamp logged Saved to Supabase instantly Engineer does nothing extra
System
📌
Check-in Record Created
Database logs: engineer name, GPS coordinates, timestamp, accuracy. This is Checkpoint 1 in the verification chain — the verified start of the working day.
site_checkins table updated Ops dashboard shows engineer as active
Radius
🚗
Van GPS — Arrival at Site
Radius Telematics records the van arriving at the site GPS coordinates. Fully automatic — no engineer action needed. This is Checkpoint 2.
Arrival time logged GPS coordinates recorded Pulled via Power Automate every hour
2
During the Day — Work Recording
Working Hours
Engineer
🔩
Opens Digital Day Work Sheet
Engineer opens skyform_dws.html on their phone. The moment the page loads, device timestamp and GPS are silently captured — this is the verified form-open time. They fill in the form exactly as they would on paper.
Open timestamp: immutable GPS on open Identical to paper DWS layout Works offline — syncs when online
Engineer
🔧
Logs Work Per Turbine
For each turbine: enter the turbine number, time started, and description of work. Add parts used with serial numbers. Mark each part as supplied by Skyform or client.
Per-turbine work log Parts + serial numbers Supplier attribution (Skyform/Client)
3
End of Day — Submission and Signatures
Close of Day
Engineer
✎
Engineer Signs and Submits
Engineer draws their signature on the touchscreen and taps Submit. Submit timestamp and GPS are captured at this exact moment — Checkpoint 3. The form is immediately locked and sent to the database.
Submit timestamp captured GPS on submit Signature image stored Record locked — cannot be edited
Engineer
🔑
QR Card Departure Scan
Engineer scans their card again on departure from site or pier. GPS and timestamp logged — this is Checkpoint 4 and the verified end of the working day.
Departure GPS + timestamp Total verified time on site calculated
System
🔒
Unique Client Link Generated
The system automatically generates a unique, one-use counter-signature link tied to this specific sheet. The engineer copies it from the confirmation screen and sends it to the windfarm supervisor via WhatsApp or email.
Unique ref per sheet Expires after signing No client account needed
4
Client Counter-Signature
Client Action
Client
📱
Client Opens Link on Their Device
Windfarm supervisor taps the link on their own phone or tablet. They see a clean summary showing exactly what work was done, which turbines, which parts were used — identical to the paper DWS they receive today.
No app, no account, no download Batch signing — multiple days at once Client sees only work data — no GPS/tracking info
Client
✎
Client Signs and Submits
Client draws their signature and taps Confirm. Their signature, name, and date are sent back to the database. Critically, the timestamp comes from THEIR device — completely independent of the engineer's submission.
Independent device timestamp Independent GPS (if permitted) Second, independent record of signing time
5
Automatic Verification — Ops Dashboard
Intelligence Layer
System
⚙
Radius Telematics Cross-Reference
Power Automate pulls journey data from the Radius Kinesis API every hour. For each submitted sheet, the system matches van arrival and departure GPS against the claimed site and times. Discrepancies surface automatically.
Van arrival vs claimed on-site time Van departure vs claimed off-site time GPS site match
Ops Manager
📊
Live Dashboard — Green / Amber / Red
Every sheet in the live viewer shows an automatic confidence score. Green means all data points align. Amber means client signature is pending. Red means there is a discrepancy between submitted times and verified data — and exactly what the discrepancy is.
Green: All checks passed Amber: Awaiting signature or Radius data Red: Anomaly detected — review required Full audit trail retained permanently

What this means in practice
🗃
No more paper
The digital DWS replaces paper completely. No lost forms, no illegible handwriting, no chasing engineers to return sheets.
🔒
Verified hours
Six independent data points corroborate every sheet. Discrepancies surface automatically — no one needs to look for them.
⏳
Faster payment
Sheets are submitted in real time. Green sheets can be invoiced immediately. No waiting for paper to come back to the office.
🙏
Client experience unchanged
The client sees exactly what they see on paper today. They review the work and sign. Nothing else changes for them.
🌎
Offshore-ready
Works offline. QR scan at the pier captures the verified start and end of every offshore deployment regardless of connectivity.
📊
Live ops intelligence
Ops manager sees every engineer, every site, every sheet in real time. Anomalies are flagged before payment is processed.
